Introduction to Web Development Ethics

First, how do we define the word "Ethics?"
Ethics is a system of moral principles. In short, it is what is good and bad.
What is Web Devolpment
Web development is the building and maintenance of websites. It enhances a user's experience.
What is Web Development Ethics?
Web Development Ethics is the discipline or the moral standards in creating webpages or websites.
What is the purpose of Web Development Ethics?
Its purpose is to remind developers that each design is made not only for the user but also to fellow developers.
Why is Web Development Ethics important?
Ethics can help ensure the longevity of our websites and help both the users and developers involved.

What are some questions that are addressed when applying the Web Development Ethics?

  1. What world / future are you building for the end-user?
  2. What type of person do you become in the process?
  3. What norms and expectations are you establishing?
  4. What are the consequences of your actions?
