Important Take-aways

  1. Web designs should support and value human rights, dignity and privacy.
  2. Ethical thinking should be promoted on online communities concerning web development.
  3. Disabled people like people with learning disorders or people who have eyesight problems should also be catered to.
  4. Inclusivity should be one of the top priorities of a developer
